Color coding of rails and roads on maps:

      
Light magenta
road, based on Google mapping technics to find the route
      
Yellow 6
tunnel or bridge [Not possible with this information to say more]
      
Violet
bank or tunnel [Not possible with this information to say more]
      
Red 3
standard gauge railway, possibly high speed
      
Light red
railway bank, with walls around and near sea level. High speed standard gauge railway.
      
Green 8
bridge. High speed standard gauge railway.
      
Yellow
tunnel. High speed standard gauge railway.
      
Black
existing broad-gauge railway
      
Blue gray
planned broad-gauge railway

By clicking on pads on the map an info window will open tells about the name of the target and coordinates of the target.

By clicking on marked (colored lines) rail and roads a info widow is opened about the target. In two cases (Kapellskär-Karis) and (Jorvas-Hiiu) every component has a spesic code from K10 forwards, this maybe helpfull for understanding how the railway is supposed to be built.
Note all info windows presents information in the language of the main window
