Jorvas

Jorvas (marked with "G") is the hub for all the connections to west, east and south. Proposals [1]( Car shuttle/Jorvas - Tabasalu ) and [3]( Jorvas - Tallinn ) are starting from Jorvas station. The standard gauge railway proposals are more problematic because it can always be argued that "we do already have a railway link, why should we build more ?"

Note the "Express to Reval" shortcut at Porkkala peninsula, which shortcuts Jorvas and Kyrkslätt stations.
